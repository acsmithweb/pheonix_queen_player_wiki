Stores and Taverns


Tavern Of The Three Moons

An upscale establishment in the town of Cledo, known for its lively atmosphere and exceptional service. Run by the charismatic Elven barkeeper Turner, the tavern serves as a popular gathering place for diplomats and the wealthy elite of the region. It offers a wide selection of fine spirits and beverages, along with delectable cuisine prepared by skilled chefs. The tavern exudes an air of sophistication and elegance, with tasteful decor, comfortable seating, and a cozy ambiance. Patrons can enjoy engaging conversations, forge business connections, and immerse themselves in the vibrant social scene of the town.


Golem's Forge

The air is permeated with the scent of metal, oil, and faint traces of enchantments. The interior is well-lit by a combination of oil lamps and strategically placed windows. The space is organized and tidy, with shelves neatly displaying various golem components, and enchanted materials.

  • Golem Limbs, Oil, Crystals Assorted limbs, torsos, and heads crafted for creating golems of various sizes and types.
  • Golem Customization Services: Skilled artisans offering to customize or upgrade existing golems, tailoring them to specific needs or preferences.
  • Golem-Themed Jewelry and Art: Rings, amulets, and bracelets adorned with golem motifs.

Points of Interest


Scarwood Castle

One of the first of several dwellings built for the Fire Fey royalty. It was first built towards the end of the Fey-Dragon war and had defended the surrounding settlement from a Blue Dragon whose lightning breath caused intricate burns into the wood of the castle giving it its name today.



Temple of Clovoni

The temple exudes a peaceful and serene atmosphere, with soft candlelight illuminating the sacred space. The air is filled with the gentle scent of incense, and the sound of quiet prayers and soothing hymns can be heard. The clergymen of Clovoni, led by Antoine Von Barentone, dedicate themselves to healing the sick and providing solace to those in need. They offer their services selflessly, spreading the message of compassion and hope in a community that often faces hardship. The Temple of Clovoni stands as a beacon of light and comfort in the midst of the slums, a place where the weary and downtrodden can find solace and healing.



House of Gellasey

The House of Gelasy is a sacred place dedicated to the worship of the goddess Gelasey. It is a grand and ornate establishment located in the heart of Cledo. The temple exudes an air of elegance and mystique, with intricate carvings and embellishments adorning its exterior. Inside, the temple is adorned with beautiful tapestries, stained glass windows, and golden ornaments, creating a visually captivating atmosphere. The clergywomen of Gelasey, known as the Daughters of Gelasy, can often be seen within the temple, engaging in prayer, meditation, and other spiritual practices.



Cledo Chronicle

The Cledo Chronicles, the newspaper office in Cledo, is housed in a quaint two-story building near the heart of the town. The exterior is made of sturdy timber and features a charming facade adorned with ivy climbing up the sides. The windows are large, allowing ample natural light to filter into the office. A weathered signboard proudly displays the name of the newspaper in bold, artistic lettering.

The building itself has a historical charm, reflecting the character of Cledo. The entrance door is solid wood, worn from years of use, and a small, welcoming bell chimes softly as visitors step inside. The interior of The Cledo Chronicles is a bustling space, with a front desk for inquiries, a printing press in one corner, and various desks scattered around where reporters and editors work diligently.
