Emberhaven

Nestled against the base of the Firepeak Mountains, Emberhaven enjoys a picturesque view of the majestic peaks. A small, somewhat downtrodden village that bears the marks of a once more prosperous past. Its connection to fey magic and the ancient, now-sealed Creation Forge gives it an air of bygone significance. The village is surrounded by dense forests, contributing to its sense of isolation

Stores and Taverns

Embers Hearth

Owned by a jovial halfling named Barley Bramblefoot, the tavern is a popular gathering place where locals share stories and histories of the area.

The Enchanted Quill

A small bookstore owned by Lyra, a half-elf, containing a collection of old books, including some on fey magic and local history.

Glimmer n Gold Trinket Shop

An eccentric store run by a gnome named Fizzlepop, selling curious trinkets and magical oddities.

Thrain's Blacksmith Shop

A humble blacksmith shop run by Thrain, a dwarf known for his traditional craftsmanship, albeit on a smaller scale than in the past.
