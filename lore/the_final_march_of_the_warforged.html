The Final March Of The Warforged

In the days when Emberhaven thrived beside the bustling Creation Forge, the village was a hub of activity and wonder. Warforged, beings of magic and metal, were crafted within the forge, each destined to protect the kingdom and serve its people. The villagers, though initially wary, grew to respect and even care for these guardians.

But peace is often fleeting. A decree from the distant capital, under the influence of court politics and fear of the unknown, commanded the sealing of the Creation Forge. The decision was met with shock and sadness in Emberhaven. The warforged, who had become part of the village's life, were to march away to an unknown fate.

On the eve of their departure, the villagers gathered to bid farewell to these unlikely companions. The warforged, each bearing the crest of the kingdom and the marks of their individuality, stood silently, their expressions unreadable but their presence commanding a solemn respect.

As dawn broke, the warforged began their march. It was a procession of solemnity and dignity. The villagers lined the path, offering silent goodbyes, some touching the cold metal arms in a final gesture of farewell. Children, who had often played at the feet of these gentle giants, watched with wide, tearful eyes.

Leading the march was Sentinel, the first of the warforged created in the forge. He turned to take a last look at Emberhaven, his mechanical eyes reflecting the first light of the sun. In time, the story of the final march became a legend, symbolizing change, loss, and the end of an extraordinary chapter in Emberhaven's history.
