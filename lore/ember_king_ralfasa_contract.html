Contract of Abdication and Protection

This contract is made and entered by and between The Ember King of Attria (hereinafter referred to as “The King”), the Archdevil Ralfasa (hereinafter referred to as “the Archdevil”), and the Ice Fey of the Feywild (hereinafter referred to as “the Ice Fey”).

WHEREAS

The Ice Fey have invaded the material plane and threatened the sovereignty and safety of The King and his people;

The King desires to protect his people from the Ice Fey and other extraplanar entities;

The Archdevil possesses the knowledge and power to create a planar anchor that would prevent the Ice Fey and other extraplanar entities from invading the material plane;

NOW, THEREFORE

In consideration of the mutual covenants and promises herein contained, the parties agree as follows:

  1. The King hereby abdicates his throne and renounces all his rights, titles, privileges, and claims to the kingdom of Attria. The King shall appoint a successor of the Ice Fey's choice, who shall assume the throne and rule the kingdom in accordance with its laws and customs.

  2. The Archdevil hereby agrees to provide the King with the schematics and materials to build a planar anchor that would prevent the Ice Fey and other extraplanar entities from invading the material plane. The Archdevil shall also instruct the King on how to activate and maintain the planar anchor.

  3. The Ice Fey hereby agree to cease their invasion of the material plane and retreat back to the Feywild. The Ice Fey shall not harm or interfere with the King or his people in any way. The Ice Fey shall also respect and abide by the planar anchor and its effects.

  4. This contract shall be binding upon the parties and their heirs, successors, assigns, agents, and representatives. This contract shall not be modified or terminated except by mutual consent of all parties or by a breach of any of its terms by any party.

  5. Any party who breaches this contract shall suffer the full wrath and vengeance of the other parties, who shall be entitled to claim any remedy or compensation they deem fit, including but not limited to: damages, injunctions, specific performance, forfeiture, enslavement, torture, death, or damnation.

  6. This contract shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Hell, which shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any disputes arising from or relating to this contract.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF

The parties have executed this contract by affixing their signatures below.

The Ember King
