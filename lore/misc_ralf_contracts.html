Misc Ralfasa Contracts

  • The Ephemeral Oath: A desperate mortician and a vengeful specter.

    Terms: The mortician must perform a forbidden ritual to bind the specter to a puppet crafted in its likeness, giving the specter a taste of the living world. In return, the specter will provide the mortician with secrets from the world of the dead.

  • The Faustian Feud: Two rival alchemists.

    Terms: They agree to exchange a single vial of their most potent elixirs. However, the elixirs can only be used once, and they have no knowledge of the other's concoction. The outcome of consuming these elixirs is uncertain, creating a thrilling gamble with unknown consequences.

  • The Binding Song: A talented bard and a mischievous imp.

    Terms: The imp grants the bard the power to influence dreams and desires through their music. In exchange, the bard must occasionally use this power to create beautiful and haunting melodies that captivate the hearts of the masses.

  • The Shifting Identity: A shape-shifting thief and a cursed noble.

    Terms: The thief will assume the cursed noble's identity for a year and a day, facing the noble's enemies and responsibilities. In return, the noble gains the thief's ability to change forms but must perform one good deed each day.

  • The Timeless Accord: An aging mage and a timeless spirit.

    Terms: The mage grants the spirit a single day of mortal existence in the material plane, during which they can experience all that the world has to offer. In return, the spirit imparts a timeless memory to the mage, preserving their most cherished moment for all eternity.

  • The Enchanted Kinship: A lonely werewolf and a reclusive witch.

    Terms: The witch agrees to craft an amulet that allows the werewolf to retain their human mind during transformations. In return, the werewolf will protect the witch's forest from those who seek to harm it. This contract forms a unique bond between the two, as they share their dual natures.
