Contract: Ralfasa and Sasori

Parties

  • Ralfasa, a devil of the Nine Hells, acting in his own name and interest, hereinafter referred to as the Patron.
  • Sasori, a human Scholar of the kingdom of The Grand King, acting in his own name and interest, hereinafter referred to as the Client.

Terms

The Patron agrees to provide the following services to the Client:

  • The Patron will grant the Client the power of strong foresight, allowing him to anticipate and react to future events. This power will last until the contract is terminated.
  • The Patron will protect the Client from any harm or danger that might threaten his life or well-being, as long as the Client follows the Patron’s guidance and instructions.

The Client agrees to provide the following services to the Patron:

  • The Client will use his power of foresight to explore the possible outcomes of his actions and decisions, and choose the ones that align with his own values and ideals.
  • The Client will follow the Patron’s guidance and instructions, as they are meant to help him achieve his desired future and avoid any pitfalls or dangers. The Client trusts the Patron’s wisdom and foresight, and does not question or hesitate to act on his advice.
  • The Client must provide Ralfasa tribute in the form of knowledge, secrets, and artifacts on a regular basis to maintain this arrangement.
  • The Client will not reveal the existence or nature of this contract to anyone, except with the Patron’s consent.

Duration

This contract will take effect immediately upon signing and will remain in force until one of the following conditions is met:

  • The Client fulfills all his obligations to the Patron and receives all his benefits from the Patron.
  • The Client breaches any term of this contract and fails to remedy it within 24 hours.
  • The Client dies or becomes incapacitated.
  • The Patron releases the Client from this contract by mutual agreement.

Consequences

If this contract is terminated by any of the conditions above, except for mutual agreement, the following consequences will apply:

  • The Client will lose all his benefits from the Patron, including his power of foresight and his protection. The Patron will also have the right to inflict any harm or punishment on the Client that he deems appropriate.
  • The Client will forfeit his soul to the Patron, who will have full rights and ownership over it. The Client’s soul will be transported to the Nine Hells, where it will be tortured and transformed into a lemure, a mindless slave of the devils.
  • The Patron will have no further obligations or liabilities towards the Client.

Signature

By signing this contract, both parties acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agreed to all its terms and conditions. They also affirm that they are acting freely and willingly, without any coercion or deception.
