Soul Pact Between Brutus and the Arch Devil

Party of the First Part (Brutus):

  • A Black-Furred Tabaxi named Brutus, of sound mind and body, henceforth referred to as "Collector."

Party of the Second Part (Arch Devil):

  • Ralfasa, A powerful Arch Devil, henceforth referred to as "The Infernal Patron."

Terms and Conditions:

  1. The Pact: Collector agrees to offer 1/14th of their own soul in exchange for the following abilities and benefits granted by The Infernal Patron.

  2. Soul Collection: Collector gains the ability to collect the souls of creatures. For every four souls collected, one soul shall be retained by the Collector.

  3. Favors and Boons: Collector may exchange the retained souls for favors, magical items, knowledge, or other rewards as determined by The Infernal Patron. The value and nature of such exchanges are at the discretion of The Infernal Patron.

  4. Temporal Limitation: The Pact shall remain in effect until such time as The Infernal Patron releases Collector from their obligations or for the duration of Collector's mortal existence, whichever comes first.

  5. Consequences of Breach: Breach of this Pact, including failure to provide the promised souls or refusal to fulfill an agreement with The Infernal Patron, will result in the forfeiture of Collector's entire soul.

  6. Binding Nature: This contract is binding, irrevocable, and enforceable by cosmic forces of balance. Collector shall undertake their obligations under the agreement in a manner consistent with the code of moral complexity and redemption.
