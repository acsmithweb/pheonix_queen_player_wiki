Turner Silverwood

Race: Elf

Occupation: Bartender

Alignment: Chaotic Good

Description: Turner is a charming and charismatic individual who enjoys engaging in conversation with his patrons

Background

Turner had gained ownership of Tavern Of The Three Moons in a high stakes card game with a wealthy resident mechant. From there he converted the atmosphere of the tavern from snooty high end to a upscale rustic one. Although most patrons trend towards the wealthy side Turner is not one to turn away someone looking for an affordable dish. This gives Turner an unique insight into the town gossip, news and drama. It also affords him the ear of many nobles too.
