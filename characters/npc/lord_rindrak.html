Race: Half-Orc

Occupation: Noble/Military Strategist

Alignment: Lawful Good

Description: Lord Rindrak, the noble Half-Orc, possesses a commanding presence and a strong, imposing figure. He exudes an air of physical power and resilience. His dusky green skin carries the faint markings of battle scars, a testament to his military past.

Background

Lord Rindrak comes from a noble lineage that has a storied history in the kingdom. His family's legacy is rooted in military prowess and strategic brilliance. Lord Rindrak, true to his family's tradition, served as a distinguished military commander in his earlier years before assuming his role as a noble. Known for his tactical acumen and unwavering loyalty, Lord Rindrak has earned the respect and admiration of his peers and the townsfolk.

Interactions

He was mostly content with Orla as Governess and has found the council she has appointed as acceptable enough.
