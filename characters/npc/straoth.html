Straoth

Race: Balor

Occupation: Demon Pirate

Alignment: Chaotic Evil

Description: A fierce and fiery warrior with a new lease on life.

Background

Appointed as Captain of the Demonwing by Demogorgan he lost interest in his duties up until his confrontation with the players. Only to find himself defeated and his essence infused with a sword.

Interactions

Demonic Influences

Kenshin has obtained the sword in which Straoth's essence is stored. With a penchant for violence he tries to encorage Kenshin towards violence.
