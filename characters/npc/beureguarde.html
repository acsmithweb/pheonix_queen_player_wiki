Beureguarde

Race: Human

Occupation: Butler

Alignment: Lawful Good

Description: A brave and loyal butler who seamlessly blends in with the city of Cledo.

Background

A friendly and kind urban druid who works to protect the city of Cledo. He blends into the city as a unasumming but loyal butler

Interactions

Murder of Warler Sr.

Upon the death of his master he worked to assist the players in getting to the bottom of the mysterious murder. This eventually led the party into one of Sasori's traps which Beureguarde protected them from resulting in his soul getting sent to hell.
