Antoine Von Barentone

Race: Human

Occupation: Clergyman/Healer

Alignment: Lawful Good

Description: A soft spoken man who is skilled in the art of healing.

Background

Antoine is a kind-hearted and compassionate man, who genuinely cares about the well-being of the sick and the poor. He is patient and understanding, and takes the time to listen to those who come to him for help. He is also deeply devoted to the teachings of Clovoni, and strives to embody the values of compassion and healing that the religion espouses.
