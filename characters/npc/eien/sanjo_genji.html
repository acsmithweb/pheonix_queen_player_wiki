Sanjo Genji

Race:Human

Occupation:Bloodlusted Swordsman

Alignment: Neutral Evil

Description: An evil swordsman who roams the Eien Kingdom masquerading as the exiled samurai Kenshin.

Background

The youngest member of the Genji clan and betrothed to a beautiful woman from one of the lesser houses. He grew up training in the martial arts alongside Kenkshin each competing for recognition. Sanjo had then realised that Kenshin had fallen in love with his betrothed who shared the same feelings. This led Sanjo down a dark path seeking power and vengeance.

Deal with the Devil

Sanjo in his fury made a pact with Ralfasa granting Sanjo the power to immitate Kenshin's appearances and go on intense massacres. One of which had led to the death of Sanjo's. Anyone slain by Sanjo's blade has their soul sent to Ralfasa himself.
