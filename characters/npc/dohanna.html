Dohanna

Race: Halfling

Occupation: Bounty Hunter

Alignment: Lawful Good

Description: A rough and tumble halfling who hunts down delinquents and ruffians while also helping the less fortunate.

Background

Due to being a halfling Dohanna typically stays to the slums of smaller cities to avoid attracting attention to herself. She focuses on helping the small city law enforcement track down wanted criminals as well as assist the downtrodden in any way she can.

Interactions

Dohanna had assisted the players in tracking down Genro before he got assasinated by the Guild of the Eclipsed rose. She also was put in charge as a member of the Governess' council.
