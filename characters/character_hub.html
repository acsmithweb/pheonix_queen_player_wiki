Eien Kingdom

Head Clans of Eien

  • Genji Clan: The Head Clan of the Eien Kingdom lead by Shogun Yoshimitsu Genji and rules from Dragonlight Citadel. The Genji clan are the designated protectors of the Planar Anchor
  • Fujiwara Clan: A clan of dwarves that lives in Forgepeak city. Known for their forge work and abjuartion magic. They are responsible for the creation and maintnence of the Planar Anchor.
  • Takeda Clan: A clan of humans who are in charge of the Eien Kingdom's maritime economy and military. They are avid explorers always looking for new places and people to interact with.
  • Oda Clan: A savage clan of Orcs and half-orcs known for their cunning yet barbaric warriors. They roam the frozen tundra of the Eien Kingdom raiding villages to survive and take their plunder back to The Frostscorched Stronghold.
  • Uesugi Clan: An orc clan that lives in the northern forests of the Eien Kingdom the Yorimori Embrace being their ancestral home. They are loyal and honorable warriors who have a reverance for nature and skilled with bow and stealth.

Wildheart Kingdom

Tribes of The Wildheart

The Sunclaw, Windfeather, Stonefang, and Moonhorn tribes not only coexist but also thrive through shared values of respect for nature and the fey. Their interaction goes beyond mere survival; they engage in extensive trade among one another, celebrate festivals, and form alliances to ward off external threats.

  • Stone Fang Tribe Thriving in the swampy realms near the rivers, the Lizardfolk tribe commands attention with green scales, and powerful jaws. Valuing strength, survival, and tradition, they engage in the trade of skins and bones while also embracing the thrill of the hunt and the intensity of battle.
  • Sunclaw Tribe Roaming the sun-kissed grasslands near the lake, the Tabaxi tribe dazzles with golden fur, and sharp claws. Adventurous and playful, they bring an air of curiosity to The Wildheart Kingdom. Their pride, competitiveness, and impulsive nature make them a force to be reckoned with.
  • Moonhorn Tribe Nestled in the embrace of the forest near the mountains, the Firbolg tribe stands out with pale skin and red hair. Devoted to peace, kindness, and the art of healing, they contribute to the well-being of the tribes.
  • Windfeather Tribe Inhabiting the lofty peaks near the forest, the Aarakocra tribe enchants the skies with their colorful feathers and graceful wings. Valuing freedom, balance, and the beauty of their mountainous abode, they engage in the exchange of messages and news with neighboring tribes and kingdoms.

Tribes of The Five Fangs

The Tribes Of The Five Fangs each worship and serve one of five Chromatic Dragons. The Fang Tribes like their patrons are in a stalemate among one another and focus on appeasing their Dragon Lords which sometimes includes harrassing the more peaceful Wildheart Tribes.
